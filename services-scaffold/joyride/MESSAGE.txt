CoreDNS-based Joyride enabled!

Containers with these labels will get DNS entries:
  - joyride.host.name=myapp.example.com
  - coredns.host.name=myapp.example.com

HOST_IP is auto-detected from the default route (host networking).

Legacy environment variables are supported:
  - JOYRIDE_ENABLE_SERF=true  (enables clustering)
  - JOYRIDE_NXDOMAIN_ENABLED=true  (return NXDOMAIN instead of drop)

Health check: http://localhost:5454/health
Prometheus metrics: http://localhost:9153/metrics

Source: https://github.com/traefikturkey/joyride/tree/coredns
